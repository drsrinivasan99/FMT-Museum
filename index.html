Welcome to the Museum of Forensic Medicine & Toxicology, AIIMS Patna. This state-of-the-art educational facility has been developed to provide an interactive and comprehensive learning experience for undergraduate and postgraduate medical students. The museum houses an extensive collection of forensic specimens, weapons, poisons, charts, murals, photographs, the Wound Man, forensic investigation kits, models, and digital exhibits that enhance the understanding of forensic medicine and toxicology. Simulated moot courts and a variety of attractive educational aids provide an engaging learning environment that bridges the gap between classroom teaching and real-world medico-legal practice.
